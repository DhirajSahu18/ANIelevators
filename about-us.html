About Us

About Us

We take pride in introducing ourselves as a leading name in the field of supply, installation, testing, commissioning, repair, modernization & maintenance of all types of lifts for the last 5 years. ANI lifts offers quality & safe products like commercial elevators, automatic lifts, capsule lifts, machine room less lifts, dumb waiters etc. ANI has expended its wings in North India and is expanding the sphere of its activities to many cities where its products are involved in transport thousands of people daily.

OUR MISSION IS TO PROVIDE CUSTOMIZE SOLUTIONS

Our designs comply with the latest safety standards and are safe, Nano technology efficient, Convenient, Powerful and tailored as per your requisites. our mission is to deliver our very best at each & every facet of the elevator business.
