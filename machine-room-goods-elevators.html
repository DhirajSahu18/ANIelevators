Goods Elevators

ANI Elevators one of the prominent manufacturers and suppliers of a wide range of Goods elevator/lift.

We use high-grade material to provide superior performance and durability.

We provide goods lifts in various sizes, capacities, and shapes so that the requirements of all our customers are fulfilled with these. There are also options for getting your goods elevator customized to meet your personalized requirements.

These goods elevators are designed and built to carry out heavy-duty lifting with speedy Jerk free smooth travel and Energy Efficient. Thereby saving Critical Fragile Material from Damage, Reducing the travel time, and Saving Energy.

To assure its flawless finish, the goods elevator are stringently tested on various quality parameters by our experts.

We have come up with the most reliable goods lift which brings efficiency to your overall operations while at the same time reducing the risk of injury to the workers.

Benefits of ANI Goods Lift/Elevators
  • Goods lift are easy to install and uses less space.
  • Our goods lift offer high-class performance. Also the maintenance requirement is low.
  • It is power efficient and thus adds to your savings.
  • These goods elevator has traction gear/gearless technology that minimizes vibration and noise.
  • Our goods lift can bear heavy loads easily making it easier and faster to work.
  • Get ready to impact your business with the most trusted ANI goods elevator/lift for the longevity of your organization.
  • Dumbwaiter / Freight Elevator is designed and built to withstand the most demanding use. Freight Elevator that we offer will provide years of reliable service under even the harshest and most challenging conditions. With years of experience and knowledge in this domain, we are offering an exclusive assortment of High Freight Elevator that is used for carrying industrial goods.
  • Freight Elevators are manufactured with load capacity of 100, 250, 500, 1000, 2000, 3200, 5000, 6300 kg.
  • They are designed for load transportation with the guide or without and for an installation in the residential buildings, enterprises like stores, shops, restaurant, etc.
